Master Thesis

2008 - 2009
Technology Evaluation for future Internet Protocol Television ServicesEricsson AB - Stockholm, Kista

Thesis aimed at evaluating Ericssons IPTV-platform and its ability to supply possible future services.

The project started out with concepting and designing a new fictitious service aimed at using the capabilities of IPTV in terms of interactivity and personalization. The service was then used as a basis for two prototypes developed using the Ericsson platform to evaluate the level of support available. The prototypes could then be used as examples for future projects on the platform.

Through out the work with the prototypes some challenges with the platform was uncovered in relation to development of new services. Among those were complexity issues in application development leading to the need of an increased knowledge base internally. Potential solutions wre presented based on level of investment required. Some suggestions included more general improvements to the platform.

The thesis also presented a model for creating smaller services to the platform called Content Interactive Widgets and how they could effect future development of the platform.
